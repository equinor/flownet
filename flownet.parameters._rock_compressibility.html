flownet.parameters._rock_compressibility module¶

class flownet.parameters._rock_compressibility.RockCompressibility(reference_pressure: float, min_compressibility: float, max_compressibility: float)¶

Bases: flownet.parameters._base_parameter.Parameter

Parameter type which takes care of stochastically drawn rock compressibility parameters. The rock compressibility will always have a Uniform Distribution between the min and max values.

Parameters

  • reference_pressure – reference pressure for rock compressibility definition

  • min_compressibility – minimum rock compressibility

  • max_compressibility – maxmimum rock compressibility
